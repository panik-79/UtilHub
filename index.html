WHY UTILHUB ?






One major problem that students face due to the non-availability of stationary and books at proper time and library is difficulty in completing assignments and preparing for exams. Without access to necessary materials, students may struggle to complete their work on time or at the level of quality expected by their teachers. This can lead to lower grades, increased stress and anxiety, and a decreased overall learning experience. Additionally, if students have to rely on purchasing their own materials, they may face financial barriers that limit their access to educational resources.

This platform will allow students to connect with each other to exchange books and stationary items for a limited period of time or they can sell it too.
